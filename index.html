Frequently Asked Questions

What is TorHub.io?

TorHub.io provides verified darknet markets & onion links for researchers. We serve cybersecurity researchers, investigative journalists, and analysts with PGP-verified marketplace mirrors and onion services. Every link is manually verified to provide anti-phishing protection for legitimate research purposes.

How do you verify onion links?

We use multiple verification methods including cross-referencing with official announcements, checking for consistent service patterns, and monitoring for phishing attempts. Every link undergoes manual review before being added to our directory.

Is TorHub.io safe to use?

Yes, TorHub.io is designed with security in mind. We use no JavaScript, no tracking, and no cookies. All links are verified to prevent phishing attacks. However, always use Tor Browser and verify addresses independently.

What does the status color coding mean?

Green indicates services are online and operational. Orange means the service is experiencing DDoS attacks or maintenance. Red indicates prolonged downtime or potential issues.

Can I submit new onion links?

Yes, we welcome submissions from researchers and journalists. Contact us at torhub_io@proton.me with verification details. We prioritize services that provide clear verification methods.

How often is the directory updated?

We continuously monitor all listed services and update status information in real-time. New services are added regularly as they are verified through our security review process.
